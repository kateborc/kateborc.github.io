Outreach

Created the EEB Spring Symposium Art Contest in 2020 to integrate art and science while encouraging participation in our research symposium. The contest displays photography and creative artwork from Iowa State's EEB Department during our annual graduate symposium. I hope to expand the art contest in the future to encourage undergraduate students in Biology to create art and attend the EEB Symposium.
