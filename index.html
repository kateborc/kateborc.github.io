About Me

I am a 2nd year PhD student in Dr. Amy Toth’s lab at Iowa State University. I currently study plant-bee pollinator networks in conservation habitat in agroecosystems. I specifically work with one conservation program called “prairie strips”, which are native prairie plant communities within rowcrop fields to improve water quality and soil retention while providing habitat for native organisms. After receiving my doctorate, I hope to become a faculty member at a research university to continue my research and encourage students to pursue careers in science and research.
