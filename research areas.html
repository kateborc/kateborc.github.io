Plant-Pollinator Communities:

Using species interactions for a systems-ecology approach to evaluate conservation habitats for community resilience, disease transmission, and rare species support. I used this approach when evaluating an increasingly popular conservation practice called prairie strips, finding beneficial wild plant-bee community functions (Borchardt et al., 2023) that were not negatively affected by the presence of a honey apiary from early to mid-summer (Borchardt et al., 2025). Currently, I am investigating the potential to design habitats that support a diverse pollinator community while decreasing a bee-related pathogen (Ongoing post-doctoral work).

Pollinator Behavior and Role:

I have investigated the pollination services, pathogen vulnerability, and foraging behavior in bees and wasps. In generalist foraging bees, I built an individual-based model to determine how past foraging experiences influence current behavior specifically regarding the number and effect of each past experience and the cost of switching between plant species. I found that the main driver of foraging behavior is the number of past experiences used to inform behavior, which causes behavior to lag behind shifts in the floral community (in prep.). In addition, I found a common bee parasite in multiple Vespid wasp species, with a small degree of cross-infectivity of the parasite originally from paper wasps and bumble bees in the other pollinator species, highlighting that wasps may play a small role in bee-related pathogen dynamics (in prep.).

Effects on Plant Pollination:

While historically assumed to be inefficient pollinators, I found wasp families fill a distinct ecological niche from bees, carry similar pollen compositions on their body to bee families, and paper wasps can deposit similar amounts of pollen in a single visit to a flower as bumble bees (Borchardt et al., 2024). Currently, I am investigating how heat stress impacts hybrid sunflower seed production and the pollinators neccessary for this process, to determine how climate change may impact this agricultural industry.
